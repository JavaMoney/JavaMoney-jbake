10th Anniversary of the Final Release

JSR 354 Final 10th Anniversary

Today marks the tenth anniversary of the JSR 354 Final Release.
The same year, Java will be turning 30, 10 days from now.
Maintenance lead Werner and EG member Simon met two days later at JCON Europe 2025, which also celebrates its tenth anniversary and 30 years of Java, to toast on the occasion with a cup of coffee.
